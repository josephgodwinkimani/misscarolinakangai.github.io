Aesthetics are an important component of any interior design project but a professional interior designer focuses on much more than appearance.



Miss Carolina Kangai is a full-service residential interior design and build firm, founded by Caroline Kangai in 2015. Through her years of experience, Miss Carolina has developed a unique style of mixing old with new and giving each project a timeless feel. Seamlessly juxtaposing limestone, marble, earthy woods, textured fabrics and organic colors with materials such as metal and glass is what gives Erica's work a striking and harmonious feel.

Miss Carolina Kangai's philosophy is to create inviting and elegant interiors-- straying from trends that will only disappoint in years to come. Our design approach is to Architecturally space plan every square inch of a room to work both aesthetically and functionally. Each project is tailored specifically to each client, as Miss Carolina pays special attention to every detail that goes into project. Known for her incredible Kitchens and Bathroom renovations, challenges such as small spaces and children's rooms are welcome and embraced by Miss Carolina and her staff, as they creatively transform some of the smallest most unusable spaces to multi-functional rooms. Miss Carolina Kangai is committed to using sustainable materials, wherever possible.
